# Parts of Speech (Grades 3-5)

## What Are Parts of Speech?

Parts of speech are the building blocks of sentences! Every word in a sentence has a job to do. Understanding these jobs helps you write better and understand what you read.

Think of it like a team: each player has a position and role!

**What they are:** Words that name people, places, things, or ideas

**Types of Nouns:**

**Common Nouns** (general)
- person, city, dog, book, happiness

**Proper Nouns** (specific - always capitalized)
- Sarah, New York, Rover, Harry Potter

**Examples in sentences:**
- The **dog** ran in the **park**.
- **Maria** read a **book** about **dinosaurs**.
- **Love** and **kindness** are important.

**Remember:** If you can put "a," "an," or "the" in front of it, it's probably a noun!
